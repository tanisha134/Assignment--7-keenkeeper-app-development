__KeenKeeper Friendship Tracker Project__

**About This Project**

This is a simple web app I built to track and manage interactions with *Friends*, *Family* *Business partners*, *colleagues*, and other *close connections*. It allows users to easily log different types of interactions such as Calls, Texts, and Video Chats.

The main goal of this project is to help users stay more aware of their communication habits and maintain meaningful relationships, By keeping track of when they last connected with someone, users can ensure they don't lose touch and can built stronger, more consistent connections over time.

*Overall, this app is designed to make staying connected simple, organized, and intentional.*

__3 Key Features Of My Project__

• *_Easy Interactions Tracking _*
*Log calls, Texts, and Video Chats interactions with your contacts in just one click.*

• *_Timeline History View_*
*View a chronological timeline of all your interactions with exact dates.*

• *_Visual Analytics Dashboard_*
*Analyze your communication habits with a pie chart showing interaction types.*
